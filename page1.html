Ginger Root Rebrand

Ginger Root is a vegan restaurant located in the Upper East Side of Manhattan. It boasts some delicious vegan dishes that are healthy and fairly priced. However, I noticed that
their restaurant identity lacked cohesiveness and recognition. Here is my concept rebrand for the restaurant.
