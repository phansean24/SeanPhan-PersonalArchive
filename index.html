Project One

For this project, I re-designed the brand identity for a vegan restaurant, called Ginger Root. Despite their delicious vegan dishes, the Ginger Root lacked a cohesive identity, and it did not portray the "lightness" of vegan cuisine.

For this project, the goal was to create a "piece of art" using elements from popular culture. I decided to design a satirical poster based on Trump and his communication habits.

This project is a parallax animation that I made using Adobe After Effects. It features a van driving endlessly through the mountains at night.
